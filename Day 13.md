# Day 13 – AI Voice Generation and Simple AI Voice Bot using ElevenLabs

## Objective

The objective of today's session was to learn how **ElevenLabs** converts text into natural-sounding speech and to build a **simple AI Voice Bot**.

## What is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is an AI-powered voice platform that converts written text into realistic human speech. It provides high-quality voice generation with different voices, languages, and customization options. It is widely used for AI assistants, customer support, audiobooks, podcasts, and content creation.

## Features of ElevenLabs

* AI-powered Text-to-Speech (TTS)
* Natural and realistic voices
* Multiple voice options
* Multilingual support
* Fast voice generation
* Easy API integration
* Voice customization

## Steps to Convert Text into Voice

1. Create an account and log in to ElevenLabs.
2. Open the **Text to Speech** section.
3. Select a voice from the available voice library.
4. Enter the text you want to convert into speech.
5. Adjust voice settings if required (such as stability or style).
6. Click **Generate** to create the audio.
7. Listen to the generated voice and download it if needed.
