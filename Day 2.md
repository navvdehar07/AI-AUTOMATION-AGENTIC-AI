# Prompt Engineering 
Day 2
## Introduction

Today I learned about **Prompt Engineering**, one of the most important skills in AI.
Prompt engineering means writing clear and effective instructions (prompts) to get the best output from AI.

A good prompt helps AI understand exactly what we want.

---

## What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt engineering is the process of designing prompts in a structured way to improve AI responses.

It helps in:

* Getting better answers
* Saving time
* Reducing mistakes
* Improving output quality

---

## Why is Prompt Engineering Important?

Prompt engineering is important because:

* It improves AI accuracy.
* It helps control the style and format of output.
* It makes automation easier.
* It is useful in AI agents and workflows.
* It reduces confusion for AI.

Without good prompts, AI may give weak or incorrect answers.

---

## Components of a Good Prompt

A strong prompt usually has these parts:

### 1. Role

Defines who the AI should act as.

Example:
Act as a teacher.

---

### 2. Task

Defines what AI should do.

Example:
Explain Python loops.

---

### 3. Context

Provides background information.

Example:
Explain to a beginner.

---

### 4. Constraints

Sets limits or rules.

Example:
Use simple language in 100 words.

---

### 5. Output Format

Defines how the answer should look.

Example:
Give answer in bullet points.

---

## Example of a Good Prompt

Role: Teacher
Task: Explain Prompt Engineering
Context: For beginners
Constraints: Simple English, short explanation
Output Format: Bullet points

Prompt:
"Act as a teacher and explain prompt engineering to a beginner in simple English using bullet points."

# Types of Prompt Engineering

## 1. Zero-Shot Prompting

### Definition

Giving instructions without any example.

### Example

Explain machine learning.

### Advantages

* Fast
* Simple

### Disadvantages

* Less accurate sometimes

### Real-Life Use

Quick information generation.

---

## 2. One-Shot Prompting

### Definition

Giving one example before asking.

### Example

Cat = Animal
Rose = Flower
Apple = ?

### Advantages

* Better understanding

### Disadvantages

* Limited learning

### Real-Life Use

Classification tasks.

---

## 3. Few-Shot Prompting

### Definition

Giving multiple examples.

### Example

Happy = Positive
Sad = Negative
Angry = Negative
Excited = ?

### Advantages

* Better pattern recognition

### Disadvantages

* Longer prompts

### Real-Life Use

Sentiment analysis.

---

## 4. Chain of Thought Prompting

### Definition

Asking AI to think step by step.

### Example

Solve 24 × 12 step by step.

### Advantages

* Better reasoning

### Disadvantages

* Takes more tokens

### Real-Life Use

Math problems.

---

## 5. Self-Consistency Prompting

### Definition

Generating multiple reasoning paths and selecting the best.

### Advantages

* More reliable answers

### Disadvantages

* More expensive

### Real-Life Use

Complex problem solving.

---

## 6. Role Prompting

### Definition

Assigning AI a specific role.

### Example

Act as a career coach.

### Advantages

* More focused answers

### Disadvantages

* Can become too narrow

### Real-Life Use

Teaching, consulting.

---

## 7. Instruction Prompting

### Definition

Giving direct instructions.

### Example

Summarize this in 5 points.

### Advantages

* Clear output

### Disadvantages

* Limited creativity

### Real-Life Use

Summaries.

---

## 8. Contextual Prompting

### Definition

Providing extra background.

### Example

I am a beginner. Explain APIs.

### Advantages

* Personalized output

### Disadvantages

* Longer input

### Real-Life Use

Learning systems.

---

## 9. ReAct Prompting

### Definition

Reason + Act approach.

AI thinks and acts step by step.

### Advantages

* Better decision making

### Disadvantages

* Complex

### Real-Life Use

AI agents.

---

## 10. Tree of Thoughts

### Definition

Explores multiple solution paths.

### Advantages

* Better complex reasoning

### Disadvantages

* Slow

### Real-Life Use

Strategy problems.

---

## 11. Meta Prompting

### Definition

Prompting AI to improve prompts.

### Example

Improve this prompt for better output.

### Advantages

* Better optimization

### Disadvantages

* Requires understanding

### Real-Life Use

Prompt refinement.
