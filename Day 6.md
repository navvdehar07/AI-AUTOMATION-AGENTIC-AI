## Day 6 - AI Study Tutor Workflow

Today we created an AI Study Tutor workflow using n8n.

[AI Study Tutor](https://github.com/navvdehar07/AI-AUTOMATION-AGENTIC-AI/blob/main/images/AI%20Study%20Tutor.png)

In this workflow, I learned:

* How to connect a webhook with an AI agent.
* How to take student questions as input.
* How to use AI to explain concepts in simple words.
* How to generate quizzes and summaries.
* How to send AI responses back using the Respond to Webhook node.

This workflow helped me understand how AI automation works in real-life learning systems. I also practiced workflow connections and debugging.
