## What is n8n Workflow?

A workflow in [n8n](https://n8n.io?utm_source=chatgpt.com) is a sequence of connected steps (nodes) that automate tasks.

Each workflow starts with a trigger and then performs actions automatically.

Example:
When a form is submitted → save data → send email → update sheet.
