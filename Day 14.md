# Day 14 – Customer Support AI Voice Bot using ElevenLabs

## Objective

The objective of today's session was to build a **Customer Support AI Voice Bot** using ElevenLabs. The goal was to create an AI assistant that can communicate with customers through voice, answer their queries, and provide instant support.

## What is a Customer Support AI Voice Bot?

A Customer Support AI Voice Bot is an AI-powered virtual assistant that interacts with customers using natural speech. It understands user queries, generates appropriate responses using AI, and replies with realistic voice output. It helps businesses provide fast and efficient customer service without requiring a human agent for every conversation.

## Features of the AI Voice Bot

* Voice-based customer interaction
* AI-generated responses
* Natural-sounding speech
* Real-time conversation
* 24/7 customer support
* Fast response time
* Easy integration with websites and applications

## Project Created

Today we developed a **Customer Support AI Voice Bot** using ElevenLabs.

### Workflow Steps

1. Create a new AI Voice Agent in ElevenLabs.
2. Configure the agent with its name and purpose.
3. Write a system prompt defining the bot's behavior and responses.
4. Select an AI model for generating answers.
5. Choose a natural voice for the assistant.
6. Configure conversation settings.
7. Test the bot with sample customer questions.
8. Improve the prompt based on the test results.
9. Deploy the AI Voice Bot for customer interactions.

## Example Customer Queries

* What are your business hours?
* How can I track my order?
* What is your return policy?
* How can I contact customer support?
* Do you offer cash on delivery?
